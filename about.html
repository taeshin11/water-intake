About Hydration Calc

A free, science-based tool to help you drink the right amount of water every single day.

Our Mission

Hydration Calc was created with a simple but powerful mission: to make proper hydration accessible, personalized, and effortless for everyone. Dehydration affects an estimated 75% of adults at some point, contributing to fatigue, poor concentration, headaches, and long-term health complications. Yet most people have no idea how much water they actually need.

We built Hydration Calc to bridge that gap. Instead of relying on the outdated "8 glasses a day" myth, our tool delivers a science-backed, personalized recommendation based on your unique body weight, daily activity level, and the climate you live in. Whether you're a desk worker in a temperate city or a marathon runner training in tropical heat, Hydration Calc gives you a number you can trust.

The Problem We Solve

Most hydration advice online is generic, inconsistent, and not backed by evidence. A 50 kg sedentary person and a 100 kg athlete clearly don't need the same amount of water — yet most resources treat them identically. This leads to two dangerous outcomes: chronic under-hydration (fatigue, kidney strain, reduced mental clarity) or, in rarer cases, over-hydration (hyponatremia).

Hydration Calc eliminates the guesswork. Our engine uses the clinically recognized baseline of 35 millilitres per kilogram of body weight, then applies scientifically validated multipliers for physical activity (ranging from 1.0× for sedentary lifestyles to 1.5× for professional athletes) and adds climate-specific adjustments (up to +500 mL for hot/humid environments and high altitudes). The result is a recommendation tailored specifically to you.

Who Is Hydration Calc For?

Hydration Calc serves a wide range of users who care about their health and wellbeing:

  • Health-conscious individuals who want a precise daily water target rather than vague advice.
  • Athletes and fitness enthusiasts who need to compensate for fluid loss during intense workouts, sports, or endurance events.
  • Parents and caregivers looking for reliable hydration guidance for family members of different ages and activity levels.
  • Remote workers and students who often forget to drink water during long focus sessions and need a visual tracker to build better habits.
  • Travelers and expats adjusting to new climates — hot, cold, or high-altitude environments all change your body's water requirements.
  • Healthcare professionals seeking a quick, shareable tool to recommend personalized hydration targets to patients.
  • Anyone recovering from illness where increased fluid intake is medically recommended.

The Science Behind the Calculator

Our calculation engine is built on peer-reviewed hydration science and follows a three-step process:

  1. Baseline calculation: We start with 35 mL per kilogram of body weight — a widely accepted clinical guideline endorsed by the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA) and consistent with recommendations from the National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine.
  2. Activity adjustment: Physical exercise increases water loss through sweat and respiration. Our engine applies a multiplier from 1.0× (sedentary) up to 1.5× (professional athletes training twice daily), reflecting the progressive increase in fluid requirements with rising activity intensity.
  3. Climate modifier: Environmental conditions play a significant role in hydration needs. Hot and humid climates increase sweat rate dramatically. High-altitude environments accelerate respiratory water loss due to lower air pressure and humidity. Cold climates also increase metabolic water demand. We add 250–500 mL depending on your environment.

The final result is expressed in both millilitres and cups (1 cup = 250 mL), making it immediately actionable. Our interactive cup dashboard then lets you visually track every glass you drink throughout the day, turning a simple number into a habit-forming experience.
